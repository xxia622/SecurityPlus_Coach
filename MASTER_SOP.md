## High-Yield Study Resources

### 1. Professor Messer (Free — Highest ROI)
- **URL:** professormesser.com/security-plus/sy0-701
- **What to use:** Full SY0-701 video course (free), practice exams, study groups
- **Best for:** Conceptual understanding, visual learners, quick domain sweeps
- **Tip:** Watch at 1.5x speed on first pass, 1x on second pass for retention

### 2. Jason Dion (Udemy / Diontraining.com)
- **URL:** diontraining.com
- **What to use:** Video course + 6 full practice exams (900 questions total)
- **Best for:** Performance-based question (PBQ) prep, exam simulation
- **Tip:** His practice exams run harder than the real exam — aim for 80%+ before sitting

### 3. Mike Chapple & David Seidl (Sybex Official Study Guide)
- **Book:** "CompTIA Security+ Study Guide: Exam SY0-701" — ISBN 978-1394211449
- **What to use:** Chapter review questions, flashcards, Sybex online test bank
- **Best for:** Deep technical reading, reference during weak-domain remediation
- **Tip:** Use as a reference, not a cover-to-cover read — go to weak domains first

### 4. CompTIA Official Labs & CertMaster
- **URL:** comptia.org/training/certmaster-learn/security
- **What to use:** CertMaster Learn (adaptive), CertMaster Labs (hands-on), CertMaster Practice
- **Best for:** Performance-based questions, hands-on terminal practice
- **Tip:** CertMaster Labs directly mirrors PBQ formats used on the real exam

### 5. Supplemental Resources
- **Anki Flashcards:** Search "Security+ SY0-701" on AnkiWeb — use shared decks
- **Reddit:** r/CompTIA — daily questions, exam reports, weak-area threads
- **TryHackMe / HackTheBox:** Hands-on labs for Domain 2.0 and 4.0 skills
- **NIST SP 800-53 / NIST CSF:** Primary framework references for Domain 5.0

## Exam Day Checklist
- [ ] Government-issued photo ID
- [ ] Arrive 30 min early (testing center) or clear environment (online proctored)
- [ ] 90-minute exam, up to 90 questions (multiple choice + PBQs)
- [ ] Passing score: 750 / 900
- [ ] Flag PBQs and return — do not spend more than 5 min on any single question
